Change Your Environment

Head to the nearest beach, lake, river, or park near water. Even sitting beside a fountain or a pond counts. Bring nothing but yourself if you can. Let your eyes rest somewhere open.

The science
Walking activates the brain's default mode network, where your best ideas arrive and your mood quietly lifts. Movement is not a reward for feeling well. It is one of the tools that helps you get there.
